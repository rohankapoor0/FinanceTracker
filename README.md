# 💸 Finlytics AI Powered Finance Tracker

A simple finance tracker that helps you keep track of your money with dates, transactions, and clear reports — so you always know where your money is going.

This project is built to make personal finance less stressful and more understandable.

---

## ✨ What it does

* 📅 Track income and expenses by date
* 🧾 Log transactions with descriptions and amounts
* 📊 Generate transaction reports
* 🔍 Easily review your financial history over time

No spreadsheets, no guesswork — just clean and organized finance tracking.

---

## 🛠️ Features

* Add income and expense transactions
* Store transactions with dates
* View all transactions in one place
* Generate simple reports (daily, monthly, or custom ranges)
* Lightweight and easy to use

---

## 📘 How It Works

1. Add a transaction (income or expense)
2. Assign a date, amount, and description
3. View your transaction history
4. Generate reports to understand your spending patterns

That’s it. Simple and effective.

---

## 🧠 Why this project?

This finance tracker was built to:

* Make money tracking less overwhelming
* Practice clean data handling and reporting
* Provide a foundation that can be expanded with charts, budgets, or AI insights later
